Annual D4BL Conferece

The D4BL conference is the heartbeat of our movement. It is a laboratory for not only new ideas around the use of data for social change, but also for experimenting with new ways to build relationships by dissolving industry silos and building points of connection to target and dismantle the proliferation of bias through research and technology.

2020:
Our 3rd annual conference to be held in December 2020, will welcome hundreds of participants, host workshops and dozens of speakers for two keynotes and eight panel sessions. This year we will also introduce two pre-conference sessions: one in partnership with the Social Medicine Consortium to convene doctors and health professionals around the growing effort to challenge the use of race based risk assessments in medicine and a pre-conference session entitled “Data Journalism for Racial Justice.” More details forthcoming.
