FAQ Webpage D4BL Conference III December 2020

Is the conference still happening? At this time we are moving forward with the conference as planned and taking into consideration the health and safety of everyone involved. If this changes, we will be in communication with you. Please be sure to sign up for our newsletter at: http://d4bl.org/ so you don’t miss any updates.

When and where is the conference? December 11-13, 2020 at the MIT Media Lab, Cambridge, MA

When do the tickets go on sale? In September.

How much are tickets? $300 general admission tickets and $75 student tickets

What does the ticket price include? Is this the same for students? Tickets include access to all conference panels/workshops (more details to come as programming is finalized), and  meals: Friday evening, Saturday breakfast and lunch, Sunday breakfast and lunch.

How will I be notified when tickets go on sale? You can register for our newsletter (via our website http://d4bl.org/), you'll receive notice once registration is opened.

Is there an RFP? The deadline for RFP was March 30. We are no longer accepting proposals.

I am interested in sponsorship possibilities. Who do I reach out to? Thank you for your interest. Please email conference@d4bl.org and we will be in contact with you.

I’d like to support others who would like to attend as a scholarship sponsor or support the fund to provide travel, accomodation and child care assistance to ensure our conference supports the broad range of the socio economic levels our community represents. Where can I donate? Please visit our D4BL III Scholarship Fund.

I’d like to volunteer, where do I sign up? Thank you for your interest in supporting the conference by volunteering. Please fill out this form. Please note, we will be starting volunteer outreach in August and you will receive more detailed information at that time.

Do you offer scholarships? We do offer full and partial scholarships for travel, lodging, childcare, and some meals. Please fill out this form. You will be notified by August 15th.
